About Us

Welcome to UnaCares Clothing Initiative at IIIT Una College, a shining beacon of warmth, hope, and compassion within our beloved community. As we embark on this journey, we extend our heartfelt gratitude to you for joining us in our mission to make a positive impact in the lives of those who need it most.

UnaCares is more than an organization; it's a collective of students, faculty members, and individuals from diverse backgrounds united by a shared vision. Together, we are a family driven by the belief that every act of kindness, no matter how small, has the power to create a ripple of change in our society. Our initiative is rooted in the principles of empathy and community service. We stand together, embracing our differences and understanding the unique strengths we each bring to the table. We are dreamers, doers, and believers, and our collective strength lies in our unwavering commitment to making a difference.

Our story begins with the understanding that in the pursuit of knowledge, we are empowered to uplift those around us. At IIIT Una College, we cultivate not only academic excellence but also a sense of social responsibility. Our journey with UnaCares started with the simple recognition that a warm coat, a fresh set of clothes, and a sense of dignity could change lives.

We invite you to become a part of this journey. Whether you are a student, a faculty member, or a concerned community member, you have the power to make a difference. Your contributions, whether in the form of clothing donations, volunteer hours, or sharing our cause, are invaluable in creating a more compassionate and caring society. As Mahatma Gandhi once said, "You must be the change you wish to see in the world." By joining UnaCares, you are embodying this change, and together, we can create a better world, one garment at a time, one act of kindness at a time.
